Home Alone

Each year I create a fun themed Christmas cake (apart from when I stabbed santa) and my family just love it. The designs seem to have got more and more elaborate each year and last year I nailed it. I decided to do my favourite Xmas film, Home Alone. Complete with gingerbread house & 'glass' windows, it not only has all the characters, I created the tree house with zip line, ice on the ground, the plumbing van and the steps to the downstairs basement with gun poking out of the cat flap. The front door has the hot doorknob, one burglar has no shoes and the other has a burnt head covered in feathers!

Another imperfectly perfect Christmas cake loved by all.
