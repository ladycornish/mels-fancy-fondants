CSI 'Body In A Pond' Retirement Cake

My previous career was spent as a Crime Scene Investigator for 18 years and I saw a few bosses retire during that time. This was my last boss's retirement cake which involved him being a CSI diver (he loved diving as a hobby), this time diving in to a pond to search for a body! I created the Major Incident Van, some extra CSI helpers and of course, evidence! He loved it!

Another imperfectly perfect cake loved by all - and tasted so good too!
