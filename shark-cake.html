Shark vs Snorkeller Cake

My husband was turning the big '5 0' and so I decided to see how quickly he could swim lol. He simply loves to snorkel and get as near to the big fish as he can so it was easy to decide on a theme. Here I have him about to be eaten by a shark, maybe he will get to the desert island in time?
I loved making this one, and the palm tree was particlarly tricky to create, keep safe and then construct on the day. However it all worked briliantly, nothing broke and inside was his favourite chocolate cake. Happy husband!

Another imperfectly perfect cake loved by all.
